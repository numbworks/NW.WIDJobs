Senior Manager, Sales Force Excellence

Location: 

Humlebæk, DK

Job Family:  Marketing
Country/Region:  Denmark
Senior Manager, Sales Force Excellence 

Sales Force Excellence (Senior) Manager for our Consumer sales channel and consumer teams

 

Want to use your sharply honed business acumen and aptitude for change management to grow a commercial mindset in our consumer teams and make sure they are ready to help our end users get the best possible service?

 

In this position, you get to grab every tool in your well-equipped commercial toolbox and put them to good use as you work with consumer care managers across the globe. 

 

“Navigating in an international setting, you will be instrumental in ensuring the quality with which we handle consumer engagement and consumer sales within our markets,” explains Global Head of Sales Force Excellence Mette-Katrine Ejby Buch. 

 
Join Sales Force Excellence and link up with our markets
At our HQ in Humlebæk, you will join our Sales Force Excellence team which is part of Commercial Operations. Here, our team of 9 colleagues work to support all subsidiaries with development and quality in customer engagement both in terms of professional sales and sales directed at the consumer.


Driven by a desire to develop and ensure a high level of quality in our customer engagement, we welcome you to a dynamic setting where we work closely with our markets across the globe. 


“Each is in charge of own area of expertise, but as a team, we also supplement each other well as we work cross-functionally to achieve the best results,” Mette-Katrine Ejby Buch finishes. 

 

Ensure high quality service and sales 
You will be responsible for driving the change management agenda for our Consumer channel. Doing so, you will be supporting our consumer care managers and consumer specialists across the globe in their daily task of locally servicing and selling products to our end users. In short, it will be your task to support development of various consumer teams across the globe and cultivate a sales mindset to go along with their empathic approach to our end users. 

 

Also, you will set out to build training modules when a new product is launched or a new marketing campaign sets sail so that our consumer teams can provide the best guidance. This means working closely with colleagues across HQ as well as within Marketing.

 

More specifically, your key tasks will be to:

  • Set a clear direction for sales capabilities within the Consumer channel, ensure that we have the right toolbox in place for in- and outbound calls and that these align with HQ requirements
  • Ensure that we locally have the tools necessary to give our customers get the highest level of quality in terms of service and sales 
  • Work with stakeholders to identify training needs and opportunities and determine what areas should be included in training modules 
  • Motivate and develop consumer care managers 


Commercial mindset and excellent communication skills
Perhaps you have worked with marketing and know the intimate health care industry inside out. You could also have worked with sales training, or maybe you have worked with change management as a consultant or something else entirely.

 

Whatever your background, you will need to excel at communication and at stakeholder management as you will be setting the direction mainly from afar. You know the importance of an understanding approach to the end user while at the same time bringing your business acumen into play when thinking ahead. It is also important that you know how to work through networks and how to motivate others. 

 

Moreover you:

  • Have a couple of years of experience in a commercial role
  • Know your way around change management
  • Probably have training experience preferably from a sales or service organisation 
  • Have impactful PowerPoint presentation skills and the ability to conduct meetings and workshops 
  • Are fluent in English – any other languages are a plus

 

Interested? 
If you would like to know more about the position, please call Global Head of Sales Force Excellence Mette-Katrine Ejby Buch at +45 4911 3358 or dkmbku@coloplast.com. 

We will take candidates into the recruitment process continuously, so do not hesitate to apply. This position will remain open until a suitable candidate has been found.
 
 

 

Pursuing an ambitious growth agenda, Coloplast develops and markets products and services that make life easier for people with intimate healthcare needs. Employing about 12.000 people and with products available in more than 143 countries, we are one of the world´s leading medical device companies. We are constantly growing our business and always looking for new ways to move forward – we explore, learn and look for new ways of doing things.

 

Coloplast is committed to being an inclusive organization, where people bring their differences to work each day, fulfil their potential and have a strong sense of belonging because – and not despite – of their differences. We therefore encourage all qualified candidates to apply regardless of gender, age, race, nationality, ethnicity, sexual orientation, religious belief or physical ability.
